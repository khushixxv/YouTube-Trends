About

Our project, YouTube Trends, aims to look at a dataset containing the top 200 YouTube videos daily in 11 different countries. By utilizing our data visualizations, both consumers and creators will be able to use this data to have a deeper understanding of how trends grow and spread on YouTube, and how to manipulate these trends.

We aim to visualize popular social media trends across time and location, show how trends change over the months, and how trends are different across countries. The project will demonstrate popular YouTube categories that receive the most attention, and people can utilize this to grow their own channels' viewer count. They will be able to understand what kinds of trends are impactful and which ones are just fads.

By looking at the rate of change and spread of different trends can give valuable insights for on what trends should be paid attention to.
